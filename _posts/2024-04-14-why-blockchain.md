# Why Blockchain Development

Changing direction at a stage in life where one has already accumulated significant experience and time requires immense courage. It means letting go of everything built so far, including the compounding benefits that come with it.

My life as a manufacturing engineer was not bad at all. In fact, it is a field where experience becomes a powerful asset over time, which made the decision to step away even more difficult. However, I chose to turn the wheel and redefine my destination for one reason: I did not want to miss this emerging transformation—one that goes beyond greater rewards and reshapes the entire global financial infrastructure.

Bitcoin, as a powerful energy-based asset, forms a solid foundation. On top of it, countless altcoins act as highways, and on those highways will flow every form of valuable asset across the globe. The idea that I could contribute to this global financial circulatory system was something that truly excited me.
